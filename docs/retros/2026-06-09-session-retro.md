### Hook-protocol friction

- **TTL expiries on architect plus JTBD gates during multi-hour finalise**: both gates' 3600s TTL expired multiple times during the long publish-day session, each requiring a refresh round-trip (architect refresh plus JTBD refresh) before the next edit could land.
