Voice guide gains a banned-patterns row for ambiguous link text ("here", "click here", "read more", "this", "more info"). Reason: out of context these links convey nothing, fail WCAG 2.4.4 (Link Purpose in Context), and read as a flat list of "here, here, here" to screen-reader users navigating by link list. Surfaced after Tom flagged three "(here)" links in the AI-agent-deleted-production article and we replaced them with descriptive link text on the topic phrases.
