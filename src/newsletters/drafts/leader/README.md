# Leader drafts: The Shift

Drafts of The Shift, the weekly newsletter targeting Engineering Leaders (JOBS_TO_BE_DONE.md J1-J4).

Filename: `<publication-date>/<publication-date>.md`. Per ADR-040, each draft edition lives in its own per-date sub-directory holding the brief plus its companion siblings, mirroring the ADR-039 published-side layout. Generated by `/wr-newsletter persona=leader` (default if no persona arg given). Persona config: `.claude/skills/wr-newsletter/personas/leader.md`.

When published to LinkedIn, move the whole draft sub-directory into the published tree: `git mv src/newsletters/drafts/leader/<publication-date>/ src/newsletters/published/leader/<publication-date>/` (per ADR-040, a single whole-directory move carrying the brief plus its sibling artefacts `<publication-date>.reviews.md`, `<publication-date>.linkedin.md`, `<publication-date>.capture.md`, `<publication-date>.cover.svg`, `<publication-date>.cover.png`).
