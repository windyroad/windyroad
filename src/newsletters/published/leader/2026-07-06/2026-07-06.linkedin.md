The model flip-flopped this week. Your talent and payoff shouldn't.

A government switched a frontier model off, then back on again in under three weeks. Fable 5 (the model some of you knew as Mythos 5) is back, but the lesson isn't the drama. The model layer keeps lurching on decisions you don't make, so don't let it set your pace. Steady the decisions that compound instead.

Inside Issue 12 of The Shift:

• The AWS CEO is hiring roughly 11,000 interns and juniors on purpose. Cutting the bottom rung isn't a saving, it's a bet you will never need the seniors it grows.

• Everyone is counting AI usage; almost no one can show the money came back. Make each initiative prove a number you can bank, revenue earned or cost removed, not seats filled or tokens burned.

• New frontier models now ship through a government licensing scheme whose rules are still being written. Don't put an unreleased model on your critical path; be patient and build on what you can run today.
